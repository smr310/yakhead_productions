About Us

Our Company

YakHead Productions is a company that carries out challenging and unique projects. We put adventure, extreme sports, photography, and technology in a blender and serve you the juice.

We take the essence, throw away the bland and get to work. Whether it's making media – extreme sport videos, still and video art works and original commercials – or consulting/planning special long-term projects and adventures, we’ll be there.

Covering every aspect of the job from top to bottom, we are the address for your wildest ideas. Reach out to us and you will see for yourself. An organic team that is a mixture of professionalism, perfectionism, reliability, quality and a bit of seasoning.!

Our Story

YakHead Productions was founded during an adventure across China. Four friends with overwhelmingly more enthusiasm than experience decided to travel across China with two motorcycles and a Land Cruiser. Without further planning, plane tickets were bought and all that was left was….well...everything else. Two months later, packed with gear they set out on their adventure.

Finding two solutions to every half problem and documenting everything while doing it, they realized they make a pretty good team, and decided to take it to the next level. They have since continued working together to help others turn dreams into reality.

Today our team is based in the U.S., Israel, and China. Our goal at YakHead Productions is to produce the highest quality photography, video and other dreams-to-reality projects with our friends around the world.
